6.3.1 Platform management

The ITS-SU shall allow for authorized remote systems to manage its operational platform to ensure expected operations.

EXAMPLE   A traffic management centre acting as a translator can reboot the unix platform of one of its roadside devices.

The ITS-SU shall allow for authorized remote systems to manage operations of installed application software to ensure expected operations.

EXAMPLE   A traffic management centre acting as a translator can control the broadcast of ad-hoc rules via roadside equipment.

ITS-SUR-14

6.11.2 Response time definition

For a request-response exchange, the response time is defined as the time from the receipt of the last byte of a request to the start of the transmission of the first byte of the response (when access is allowed by lower layers). For a publication to a subscription or broadcast, the response time is defined as the time from the information first becoming valid for publication to the respective user to the start of the transmission of the first byte of the transmission (when access is allowed by lower layers). The length of the transmission will be dependent upon the amount of data that needs to be transferred and the effective data rate of transfer.
